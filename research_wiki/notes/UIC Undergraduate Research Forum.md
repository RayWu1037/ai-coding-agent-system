# UIC Undergraduate Research Forum

## Summary

The UIC Undergraduate Research Forum is a campus venue where undergraduate students present research and scholarly work to a broad academic audience. Students submit a short abstract, and the format is well suited to project-based work that combines engineering practice with reflection on method and results. This source is useful for our project because it is a realistic near-term presentation venue for an undergraduate systems-and-AI workflow case study.
