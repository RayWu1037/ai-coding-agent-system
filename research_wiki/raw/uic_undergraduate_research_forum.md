# UIC Undergraduate Research Forum

The UIC Undergraduate Research Forum is a campus venue where undergraduate students present research and scholarly work to a broad academic audience. Students submit a short abstract, and the format is well suited to project-based work that combines engineering practice with reflection on method and results.

This source is useful for our project because it is a realistic near-term presentation venue for an undergraduate systems-and-AI workflow case study. It fits the current maturity of the project better than a traditional journal submission.

Official source: https://undergradresearch.uic.edu/urf/
