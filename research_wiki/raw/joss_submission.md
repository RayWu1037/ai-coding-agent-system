# JOSS Submission Requirements

The JOSS submission guide explains that research software should be open source, publicly hosted, documented, tested, and accompanied by a short software paper in the repository. It also makes clear that public development history and evidence of real research use matter in the review process.

This source is useful for our project because it explains why the repository is not yet ready for immediate JOSS submission. It supports the strategy of strengthening public development history, releases, evaluation, and packaging before aiming at a journal-style software venue.

Official source: https://joss.readthedocs.io/en/latest/submitting.html
